üí∞ Life Expectancy vs GDP per Capita

Discover the relationship between economic prosperity and health outcomes. Point size represents population. Use the legend to filter by continent.

Insight: Notice the strong correlation between GDP per capita and life expectancy, with notable outliers representing unique socioeconomic conditions. A clear logarithmic relationship exists‚Äîeach doubling of GDP per capita is associated with significant gains in life expectancy, though diminishing returns appear above $30,000 per capita. Countries like Costa Rica and Cuba achieve high life expectancy with relatively modest GDP.
